Welcome to the Mass Academy Rkhive

In response to the challenges created by the COVID-19 pandemic, the Mass Academy Rkhive aims to set in place a repository of Mass Academy history, including information about past students and events, to serve as a resource for past, present, and future students to connect with and learn more about the school.




Look Into the Past

Rkhive contains a high school student profile of MAMS graduates beginning with the Class of '21.

Connect with Alumni

Rkhive offers a community uniting past and current MAMS students with opportunities and support.
